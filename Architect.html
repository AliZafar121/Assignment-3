Architect

An architect is a professional who designs and plans buildings, ensuring they are functional, safe, aesthetically pleasing, and sustainable. Architects blend creativity with technical expertise to shape the built environment.

Key Responsibility

  • Design
    Creating building plans and 3D models.
  • Planning
    Planning: Collaborating with clients to meet their needs and budget
  • Construction Oversight
    Ensuring projects are executed as per design and safety standards.
  • problem-solver
    Addressing structural, environmental, and functional challenges.
  • sustainabiity
    Incorporating eco-friendly and energy-efficient designs
